Why Atlántico?


I am an Atlantic person, I was born in La Coruna, an Atlantic city, and I currently live in Ireland; the Atlantic has played a huge connection role between these two places and so it does for me too.

In Ireland I grew up as a yogi, practicing in different places and with many gifted teachers and certified as a Yoga teacher at the lovely Yoga room with Ciara Cronin.

In yoga I find an invaluable tool to feel optimal physically and nurture my mental well-being, so important. It brings balance, calmness, strength, awareness, connection, trust…

What to expect in my classes?

My classes are designed to start with gentle moments of appreciation for the breath,
in this way getting connected to ourselves and our bodies. Afterwards with dynamic movement in
coordination with the breath we create natural heat and openness across the body. There is always a
closing sequence and a moment of relaxation, a key part of the class where you absorb the benefits of the practice.

Behind Atlantico


My name is Pepa Pérez, I was born in A Coruña, an Atlantic city in the north of Spain and I currently live in Ireland. The Atlantic has played a huge part in the connection of these two places and so it does for me too.

In Ireland I developed as a yogi, practicing in different places and with many gifted teachers like Orla Fitzgerald, David Collins y Paula Herbert. I also had the opportunity to attend workshops with Guy Donahaye and Manju Jois, among others. I then certified as a Yoga teacher at the lovely Yoga Room with Ciara Cronin.

In yoga I find an invaluable tool to feel optimal physically and nurture my mental well-being, always so important; it also brings balance, calmness, strength, awareness, connection and trust.

It is my utmost motivation and objective to share these benefits through my Yoga classes while facilitating a connection with the vast ocean of possibilities Yoga has to offer.

Discover more than just a physical practice...

What to expect in my classes?

My classes are designed to start with gentle moments of appreciation for the breath, in this way getting connected to ourselves and our bodies.
Afterwards with dynamic movement in coordination with the breath we create natural heat and openness across the body.
There is always a closing sequence and a moment of relaxation, a key part of the class where you absorb the benefits of the practice.
